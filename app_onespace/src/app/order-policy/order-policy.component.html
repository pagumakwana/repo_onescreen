Shipping Policy

ONESCREEN provides digital advertising services, not physical goods. However, for marketing materials, merchandise, or promotional equipment ordered through our platform, the following policy applies:

1. Delivery Timeline

The delivery timeline refers to the ad display period selected by the customer at the time of booking on the ONESCREEN website.
Campaigns are scheduled and displayed as per the availability of the chosen advertising slots.
ONESCREEN ensures that all campaigns go live within the committed schedule once content approval and payment confirmation are completed.
ONESCREEN is not responsible for delays caused by technical issues, weather conditions, or other unforeseen operational disruptions.

2. Delivery Confirmation

Delivery confirmation occurs after the ad content provided by the customer has been reviewed and approved by ONESCREEN's content and compliance team.
Once approved, the customer will receive a campaign confirmation, including slot details, estimated display start date, and duration of the campaign.

3. Service Activation (for campaigns)

Digital advertising campaigns are considered “delivered” once the ad goes live on ONESCREEN's designated digital screens.
Clients will receive proof of play or campaign reports as per the service package.

4. Delivery Area

We currently offer campaign services across select cities in India Availability in specific regions may vary.
